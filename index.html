Here’s What You’ll Learn:

  • Trim stems at an angle for better water absorption.
  • Change water every 2–3 days to prevent bacteria.
  • Remove leaves that sit below the water line.
  • Keep flowers cool and out of direct sunlight.
  • Use flower food — or a DIY alternative with sugar, bleach, and lemon juice.
